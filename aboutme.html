About Me


Welcome to my art portfolio! I'm currently a student at EPFL.
I've been painting since november 2016, and on this website that I started during summer 2020, you will find a few works of mine that I have selected.
Painting is simply one of my hobbies, and I have been doing it on my free time since I was 13. I draw inspiration from anything really, but I feel most creative when faced with a nice landscape or when drawing inspiration from feelings and moods. I mostly make art for art's sake and try to create works that appeal to the eye.
I hope you'll find some of my pieces to your liking! If you do, feel free to contact me and let me know.
